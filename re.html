My Expperience Creating a Website

Recount Text Website

Orientation

Last month, my teacher gave me an assignment to create a simple website. At first, I felt nervous because I had never made a website before. However, I was also excited to learn something new. I decided to make a personal website that contained my profile, hobbies, and some pictures.

Events

First, I searched for information about HTML and CSS on the internet. After learning the basics, I started writing simple codes on my laptop. I created a homepage, an about page, and a contact page. Then, I added colors, images, and different fonts to make the website more attractive.


While creating the website, I faced several problems. Sometimes the layout did not look good, and some codes did not work properly. I felt frustrated, but I kept trying to fix the mistakes. I watched tutorial videos and asked my friends for help. Slowly, I began to understand how websites are made.


After finishing the design, I tested the website on my phone and laptop to make sure everything worked well. Finally, my website was completed successfully. My teacher and friends liked the result, and I felt very proud of myself.

Reorientation

Creating a website was a challenging but enjoyable experience. From this activity, I learned many new things about coding, design, and problem solving. I hope I can create a better website in the future.
